Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026  |  RBI Sathi — Independent Platform

1. Introduction

RBI Sathi is an independent social networking platform exclusively for employees of the Reserve Bank of India. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. We are not affiliated with the Reserve Bank of India in any official capacity.

2. Data We Collect

  • Mobile number — for OTP authentication only, never shown to other users
  • Official RBI email address (@rbi.org.in) — for identity verification only
  • Profile: name, date of birth, gender, designation, posting centre, department, bio
  • Profile photos uploaded by you
  • Messages sent through the platform (only to matched users)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and display your profile to eligible members
  • To facilitate introductions and messaging between matched users
  • To verify RBI employment via @rbi.org.in email domain
  • To enforce community standards and handle reported violations

4. Privacy Protections

  • Female profile photos are hidden from all users until a mutual match
  • Your full name is hidden on discovery — only first name visible before a match
  • Your mobile number is never displayed to any other user
  • Messages are only visible to the two parties in a match
  • You may delete your account and all associated data at any time

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for any commercial purpose whatsoever. Data may only be disclosed in response to a lawful legal process or court order.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. Upon deletion, all personal data is removed. Anonymised, aggregated statistics may be retained for operational purposes.

7. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete your account and all data
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

8. No Liability

The platform and its founders accept no liability for any outcome arising from use of this service. All interactions are personal and voluntary. Exercise care, good judgment, and due diligence at all times.

9. Contact & Grievances

For privacy concerns or grievances, use the Grievance option in Settings. We aim to respond within 7 working days.
